### Screen 3: 5 levels to track value

**Headline:** 5 levels to track value

**Section label:** The Journey to Proof

#### 1. Value Estimated
Cognite's initial estimate of what a use case could deliver, used for the business case.

#### 2. Customer Agreed
The customer has reviewed and agreed to the value estimate.

#### 3. Value Realized
Unilateral estimate of value already realized based on early data post-deployment.

#### 4. Value Measured
We formally measure realized value together with the customer. Results are confidential but robust.

#### 5. Value Made Public
The customer agrees to share the measured impact publicly – our gold standard of proof.

The app presents Cognite's MoonShot initiative in three paths:

1. **What is 100 × 35?** — Mission (headline, pledge line, blue card, video), real customer value principles, and the 5-level journey to proof.

3. **How we get there** — Milestones to 2035, role mandates, and customer value to date (charts + tracker).
